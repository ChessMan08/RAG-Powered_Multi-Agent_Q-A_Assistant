Q: What is the Acme Smart Speaker?
A: The Acme Smart Speaker is a voice‑controlled home assistant that plays music, answers questions, and controls smart‑home devices.

Q: Which voice assistants does it support?
A: It has built‑in support for Alexa and Google Assistant.

Q: How do I reset the speaker to factory settings?
A: Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds until the LED flashes red, then release.
